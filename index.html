Dexter


Meet Dexter, the chillest one of them all!

Dexter is a sweet 7-year-old ginger cat who loves catnip and boxes. He's very friendly and will walk up to anyone for pets. He likes to be wherever you are and will follow you if you leave the room.

Nicknames:

  • Sweet boy

Likes:

  • Temptations treats
  • catnip-infused toys
  • inspecting groceries after I go shopping

Bit


Meet Bit, the sour patch kid!

Bit is a sweet 'n spicy little girl who loves to cuddle and play. She occasionally gets grumpy, especially if you pick her up when she doesn't want to be held, but it's always shortlived. She's a silly, loving girl who loves head pets.

Nicknames:

  • Bit Bit

Likes:

Loki

Loki is a shy, silly tabby who loves to hang out with Dexter and play with Stormy. When he's not spending time with his best friend, he's usually hiding in one of his favorite spots around the house. He also likes to sit on the window sill sometimes.

Nicknames:

  • The Loke-ster

Likes:

Stormy
